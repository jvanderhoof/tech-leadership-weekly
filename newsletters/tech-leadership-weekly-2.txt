How to Deploy Software
https://zachholman.com/posts/deploying-software
Zach Holman
Read Time: 20 minutes

Maximizing the value of a development team means getting their work out and iterating as quickly and safely as possible.  Improving the deployment process can offer a high return on investment.  It results in better code, fewer production bugs, and lets a development team move faster.  This is a long post, but offers a ton of valuable considerations.  Unless releasing code is fantastically boring, your probably doing it wrong.
